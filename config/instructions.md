### 📖 How to Ask Effective Questions

To get the most accurate answers from the Clinical RAG system, follow these tips:

1. **Be Specific & Direct**: Ask clear, direct questions about clinical protocols, guidelines, or analysis models.
   - *Example*: *"What is the purpose of the ADaM standard?"* instead of *"tell me about ADaM"*.
2. **Specify Scope (Optional)**: Mention specific documents or versions if you are looking for details from a particular file.
   - *Example*: *"Summarize the inclusion criteria from the protocol."*
3. **Use CDISC Terminology**: Refer to domains, datasets, or variables using standard CDISC terms (e.g., ADSL, SDTM, DM, AE).
   - *Example*: *"What are the required variables in the ADSL dataset?"*
4. **Refine Searches**: Use the **Document Manager** to filter or select specific source documents to narrow down the knowledge base scope.
